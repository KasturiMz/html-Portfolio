Kasturi's Movie List

My Top Three Favourite Movies

The Train to Busan

It is a zombie movie which provides fine details about human emotions, feelings and instinct. It shows human reactions to specific situations.

Ratatouille

It shows a mutual relationship between a mouse and a man. It is a story portraying friendship and dependance.

Raazi

It is a real life story about a patriotic woman who gives her life to spying for her country and the struggles she faces in the whole ordeal.
